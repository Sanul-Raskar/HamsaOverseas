About Us


Hamsa Overseas is established with the objective of providing high-quality Spices, herbs, oil seeds, fresh fruits & vegetables and multiple agro products under one roof complying with export quality standards & specification of different countries across the globe. We have 3 generations of rich experience in trading and rearing farms and orchards. Implementing new technology enables us to export high quality products throughout the year.
