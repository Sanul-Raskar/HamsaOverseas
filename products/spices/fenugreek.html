Fenugreek Seeds


Botanical Name:  TRIGONELLA FONEUM-GRAECUM L

Fenugreek Seeds are small, yellowish-brown in colour, pleasantly bitter taste and a unique odour and flavour of its own.Fenugreek Seeds is rich in vitamins and minerals, and because it is a seed and a legume, hence it is high in protein.

Harvest:  January to April

Properties:  

  • Type: Sortex/ Machine Cleaned
  • Purity: upto 99.5 %

Packaging:  Jute or Paper bag
