Cinnamon


Cinnamon is one of the most popular and recognized spices in the world. Cinnamon is the inner bark of a tropical evergreen tree. Dried leaves of cinnamon, along with its dried inner bark are used all over the world as a spice or condiment. It has a pleasing fragrance and a warm, sweet and aromatic taste. The most impressive health benefits of cinnamon sticks include its ability to help manage diabetes, protect against fungal infection, increase brain function, improve digestion, and boost the strength of the immune system.

Harvest:  September to December
