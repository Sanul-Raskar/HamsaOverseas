Nutmeg


Botanical Name:  MYRISTICA FRAGRANS

Nutmeg is a smooth, dark brown nut with light brown or brown wrinkled seeds. It is about 20 mm to 30 mm (1 inch) long and 15 mm to 18 mm wide, and weighing between 5 g and 10 g. Nutmeg is used in the perfumery and pharmaceutical industries.

Properties:  

  • Moisture Content: Max 10 %
  • E. Coli: Max 3.0 MPN/G
  • Packaging: Jute or paper bag
