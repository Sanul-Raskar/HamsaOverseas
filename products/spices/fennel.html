Fennel Seeds


Botanical Name:  FOENICULUM VULGARE

Fennel seed is a highly aromatic and flavourful herb obtained from the plant of fennel. They have a sweet and refreshing flavour and emit a pleasant odour.

Harvest:  February, March

Types:  

  • Best green and largest size
  • Europe good green
  • Medium green and large size

Properties:  

  • Colour: Pale greenish
  • Purity: as required up to 99%
  • Sortex: as required up to 99.5%
  • Moisture: 10% Max
  • Volatile Oil: 2%-4%
  • Sterilization available: ETO / Steam

Packaging:  Jute or Paper bags
