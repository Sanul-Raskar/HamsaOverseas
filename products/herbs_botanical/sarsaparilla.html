Sarsaparilla (Anantmool)


Botanical Name:  HEMIDESMUS INDICUS

It is known as Indian sarsaparilla (Anantmool), is a species of plant in the Apocynaceae family. Its aromatic roots are woody and produce the stem which is numerous, slender, terete, and thickened at the nodes. It is used for making medicines and health beverages. It is available in the form of powder or tonic for the ease of consumption.
