Neem Leaves and Powder


Botanical Name:  CASSIA ANGUSTIFOLIA VAHL

Demand for Neem in the world market is growing due to increasing awareness for chemical free food and better ecological approaches to pest control along with supportive research into the use of neem extracts in Argo-Industry. Neem has also wide applications in cosmetics, food supplements, pharmacy and beverage. 100 percent natural henna is used in the manufacturing process. No chemicals are used.
